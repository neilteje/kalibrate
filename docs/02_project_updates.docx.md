# Assignment 2: Project Updates (3 total)

**Frequency:** Throughout semester at key milestones **Due Dates:** \- Update 1: March 5, 2026 (Thursday, Week 7\) \- Update 2: March 26, 2026 (Thursday, Week 9\) \- Update 3: April 9, 2026 (Thursday, Week 11\) **Submission:** PDF on Canvas (one per group)

## Overview

Share progress updates at key milestones to track your project development and maintain steady progress throughout the semester. There are 3 updates total, strategically timed to align with major project phases.

## Required Sections

Each update should include the following sections:

### 1\. Progress Summary

Briefly summarize what you accomplished this week: \- What tasks did you complete? \- What milestones did you reach? \- What concrete progress was made on benchmark or agent development?

### 2\. Current Status

Describe the current state of your project: \- **Benchmark development:** What percentage complete? What’s working? \- **Agent implementation:** What components are built? What’s functional? \- **Evaluation:** Have you run any preliminary tests?

### 3\. Challenges and Solutions

Discuss any obstacles you encountered: \- What problems or challenges did you face? \- How did you address them (or plan to address them)? \- What technical decisions did you make and why?

### 4\. Next Steps

Outline your concrete plans for the coming week: \- What specific tasks will you work on? \- What are your goals for next week? \- Are there any dependencies or blockers?

### 5\. Questions and Help Needed

Identify any areas where you need assistance: \- Are you stuck on any technical issues? \- Do you need clarification on requirements? \- Are there resources or tools you need help accessing?

### 6\. Individual Contributions

Provide a brief statement from each team member: \- One sentence per person describing what that individual did this week

## Grading Rubric (5 points per update)

* **Progress** (3pt): Did you make meaningful progress this week? Is the work substantial?

* **Clarity** (1pt): Is the update clear and well-organized?

* **Planning** (1pt): Are next steps concrete and achievable?
